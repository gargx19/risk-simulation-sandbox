💡 How It Works

This sandbox runs 1,000 Monte Carlo simulations of your portfolio across different market conditions. It calculates your median outcome and worst-case (5th percentile) scenario, then uses an AI coach (Llama 3.1) to explain the results in plain language — helping you understand risk without fear.
